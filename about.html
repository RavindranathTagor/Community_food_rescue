Mission

Our goal is to reduce food waste and fight hunger in our community. We connect surplus food with those who need it most, through the dedicated efforts of our volunteers.

Our Story

Since our founding in 2020, we've been committed to addressing food insecurity and sustainability by rescuing and redirecting surplus food to those in need.
